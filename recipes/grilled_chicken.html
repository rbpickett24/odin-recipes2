Grilled Chicken


Description


Grilled chicken is chicken meat cooked on the grill, over a fire, or with the use of a grill pan.


Ingredients

  • Vegitable Oil
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Chicken Seasoning
  • Chicken Breast

Steps

  1. Cut Chicken Breast
  2. Season chicken
  3. pour oil into pan
  4. turn the stove on
  5. plce chicken in pan
  6. cook chicken on both sides for 5-8 minutes
